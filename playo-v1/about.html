Playo-About the company

What does playo do?

Playo is a mobile app that allows users to find and book sports venues nearby, connect with other players based on their skill level and interests to play matches, and essentially acts as a platform to find playpals and organize sports activities with people in their area, all through a single app; essentially making it a social sports community platform where you can discover, book courts, and play with others. Key features of Playo: Find playpals: Search for players based on your preferred sports, skill level, and availability to play matches. Venue booking: Discover and book sports courts or facilities near you, including hourly sessions, memberships, and other booking options. Community features: Interact with other players, create teams, and chat with your group. Track your game: Keep track of your sports activities and build your profile on the platform. Access to coaches and academies: Find and connect with local sports coaches and training academies. Reviews and ratings: See reviews and ratings of sports venues and other players.
